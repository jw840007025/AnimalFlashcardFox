Fox

Foxes use their fluff tails to keep themsleves warm in freezing temperatures.

  • Scientific name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Average length: 18 to 35 inches
  • Average lifespan: 2 to 4 years
  • Habitat: mountains, deserts, forests, suburbs

Foxes are one of the most flexible creatures on Earth. They thrive in multiple habitats and eat a variety of things, such as fruits, fish, and worms. They can overcome obstacles to finding food in human environments, using their intelligence to get to an abundant supply of garbage in the suburbs.
